PyLadies Arua Refugee Python Web Development Workshop

The PyLadies Arua Refugee Python Web Development Workshop is a full-day, hands-on session designed to introduce refugees and underserved youth to Python and web development.

Organized by PyLadies Kampala, this workshop builds on the success of our 2025 bootcamp in Arua, which trained over 100 participants from refugee and university communities through practical, project-based learning.

Why This Workshop Matters

Refugee communities in Arua face limited access to digital skills training and opportunities in the tech space. This workshop aims to bridge that gap by equipping participants with practical skills that can open pathways to employment, freelancing, and continued learning.
