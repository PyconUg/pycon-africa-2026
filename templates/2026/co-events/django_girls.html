Django Girls

If you are female and you want to learn how to build websites, we have good news for you! We are holding a one-day workshop for beginners!

It will take place during the PyCon Africa 2026 conference, at Speke Resort Munyonyo, Kampala, Uganda. We would like to give you an opportunity to learn how to program and become one of us - female programmers!

The workshop is free of charge to all attendees of PyCon Africa 2026 and if you are interested in being part of this training, you can purchase a PyCon Africa Ticket and get a Pass to the Django Girls Workshop!

Welcome to the Django Girls Workshop at PyCon Africa 2026!

If you are a woman and you have a laptop - through PyCon Africa 2026, you will have the chance to meet with other women in the tech world. You don't need to know any technical stuff - The workshop is for people who are new to programming.

Django Girls Workshop FAQ

Do I need programming experience to join?

No! This workshop is for beginners. You don't need any prior knowledge. If you have some technical knowledge (like knowing what HTML or CSS are), you can still apply!

Should I bring my Own Laptop?

Yes, please bring your own laptop. We don't provide hardware, and it's important for you to have your computer to take home everything you create during the workshop.

Do I need to Install anything on my Laptop beforehand?

It would be helpful to have Python installed, but don't worry if you haven't done it yet. We'll have an installation session before the workshop to help you get set up.

Do I need to be a PyLady already to attend?

No. Simply sign up for the workshop session, and you're welcome to join other activities if you're interested.
