{% trans "Travel" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Spend a little time researching your options. If you will need to travel by air, use websites such as Skyscanner or Google Flights to estimate the cost of airfare. A little flexibility with dates or routes can make a big difference to the price of a ticket. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Accommodation" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Many sites help you estimate accommodation costs (for example, major booking platforms or hostels). Kampala offers a range of options, from budget hostels to mid-range hotels. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} The conference venue is Speke Resort Munyonyo. We recommend looking nearby or along routes with reasonable access to Munyonyo (for example areas with reliable transport links toward the lake side of the city). If you are willing to share a room to reduce costs, mention this in your application. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Be reasonable – we want you to stay somewhere clean, safe, and comfortable, but we cannot afford to place you in a luxury hotel. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Subsistence" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Lunch and refreshments will be provided each day during the conference. For evening meals and local transport in Kampala, costs vary widely; plan using your own research and local prices. Ride-hailing apps and safe taxis are widely used. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} We recommend budgeting conservatively for evening meals and transport for each day you are in the city, on top of travel and accommodation. {% endblocktrans %}
