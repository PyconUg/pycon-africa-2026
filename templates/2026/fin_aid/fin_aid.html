Opportunity Grants

Financial barriers should never stop passionate people from participating in PyCon Africa.

About the Grant

PyCon Africa 2026 is taking place in Kampala, Uganda from 7–11 October 2026. We are committed to making this event as inclusive and accessible as possible for the African Python community and beyond.

Opportunity Grants are available to help community members who would otherwise be unable to attend due to financial constraints. Grants are awarded based on need and the potential impact of your attendance on the Python community in Africa.

What Grants May Cover

  • Travel costs to Kampala
  • Accommodation during the event
  • Conference registration fee

Who Should Apply?

  • Community members who cannot afford to attend without financial support
  • Students and early-career developers passionate about Python
  • People from underrepresented groups in tech
  • First-time PyCon Africa attendees
  • Open source contributors and community organisers

Tips for a Strong Application

  • Be specific about why you want to attend and what you hope to learn or contribute
  • Explain how attending will benefit your local Python community
  • Mention any talks, workshops, or open source work you plan to contribute
  • Apply early — grants are limited and awarded on a rolling basis
