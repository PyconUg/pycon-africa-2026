Financial Aid Information - Refugee Program Python Workshop

About Financial Aid

Financial aid is available to support participants who may not be able to attend without assistance, with a focus on refugees, women, and underserved communities.

This support is intended to reduce financial barriers that often prevent talented and motivated individuals from accessing learning opportunities in tech and participating in events like this workshop.

What the Support Covers

  • Conference tickets
  • Transportation (especially for participants traveling from Arua)
  • Accommodation where necessary

Priority Will Be Given To

  • Refugees and underserved youth
  • Women and beginners in tech
  • Applicants who demonstrate strong interest but have limited access to resources

Application Process

Applicants will be required to complete a short application form, where they will share a bit about their background, interest in the workshop, and need for support.

Applications will be reviewed by the organizing team, and selected participants will be contacted with further details.

Due to limited resources, financial aid will be provided based on need and availability.

Our goal is to ensure that financial constraints do not prevent motivated individuals from participating, learning, and benefiting from the opportunities offered during the workshop and the wider event.
