PyLadies Open Source Summit

A one-day program within PyCon Africa 2026 for Python enthusiasts and open-source advocates—new and experienced—to build community and grow contributions to open source.

What to expect

  • Keynotes and collaboration with women in tech and open-source leaders from across the region.
  • Hands-on sessions: contribute to real projects, level up your skills, and connect with maintainers.
  • A welcoming space aligned with PyLadies values—everyone with an interest in Python is encouraged to take part.

Registration

Conference registration and workshop details are managed through the main PyCon Africa 2026 registration flow.

Open source maintainers

If you maintain a project and would like to mentor attendees or grow your contributor base at this summit, reach out to the PyLadies chapter coordinating the event. Selected maintainers may present projects and onboard new contributors during the program.
