Tickets

Secure your spot at PyCon Africa 2026 in Kampala, Uganda.

PyCon Africa brings together developers, programmers, DevOps engineers, scientists, and others from the Python community to discuss application techniques, updates to the language, open source development, and the Python community across Africa.

Tickets are sold via Quicket. Select the ticket type that applies to you and complete your purchase on the Quicket platform. Prices are listed in Uganda Shillings (USh).

Ticket Types

Students (Domestic and Regional)

For students within East Africa only.

Early Bird: USh 117,000

Regular: USh 136,500

Requires a valid Student ID and National ID at check-in.

Students (International)

For students outside East Africa.

Early Bird: USh 175,500

Regular: USh 214,500

Requires a valid Student ID and National ID at check-in.

Individual

For attendees who are not or no longer students.

Early Bird: USh 253,500

Regular: USh 292,500

Corporate

For teams and organisations.

Early Bird: USh 526,500

Regular: USh 604,500

Prices are as listed on Quicket. The amount charged at checkout may vary slightly.

Donate to PyCon Africa

Anyone can donate to support PyCon Africa. To donate:

  1. Click on Tickets on the Quicket page.
  2. Scroll down to find Donate to PyCon Africa.
  3. Enter the amount you wish to donate.
  4. Click Next to process payment.
